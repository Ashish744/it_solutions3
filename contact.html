Frequently Asked Questions

What industries do you serve?

We work with organizations across finance, healthcare, retail, manufacturing, education, and more. Our services are tailored to meet industry-specific compliance and security requirements.

Do you provide 24/7 support?

Yes, our managed services include 24/7 monitoring and support options. We have dedicated support teams available around the clock for critical incidents.

Can you work with our internal IT team?

Absolutely. We often co-manage environments with in-house teams, allowing for seamless integration and knowledge transfer to enhance your team's capabilities.

What makes IT Solutions different?

We combine deep technical expertise with a business-first approach. We focus on delivering measurable ROI and maintaining transparent communication throughout all engagements.

How long does a typical engagement take?

Timeline varies based on project scope. Initial assessments typically take 2-4 weeks, while migrations and implementations can range from weeks to months depending on complexity.

What should I bring to an initial consultation?

Bring details about your current infrastructure, business goals, budget constraints, and any specific challenges you're facing. We'll help you define the best path forward.
